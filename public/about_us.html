ABOUT MYSOLARPRICE.COM.AU

Mysolarprice.com.au provides a service to potential Solar power & Solar Hot water customers by aligning your specified solar requirements with accredited solar power and hot water installation companies that can meet those requirements.

HOW IT WORKS.

You enter your postcode in the postcode box in the mysolarprice.com.au page. From there you will be asked to submit some basic information specific to your needs. This should only take about a minute or so of your time.

This information is then distributed to three independent Solar companies who are willing to bid for your business. These companies will then contact you shortly after to arrange a time for an appointment or even send you a quote via email or over the phone.

WHY CHOOSE US?

We make sure that every installer on our books is accredited with the Clean Energy Council . We only deal with companies that have been trading for 2 years or more and can back up there product. We DO NOT deal with start up companies that have just started in the Solar business. We are also members of ABSA & BDAQ.
Once you have submitted your details you will be given a list of the ten most important questions that you should ask the sales rep. This is an important guide for anyone wanting to purchase solar power or solar hot water.

We will NEVER give out your details to anyone other than the Solar companies competing for your work. Mysolarprice.com.au takes your privacy seriously.
